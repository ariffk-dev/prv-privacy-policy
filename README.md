# Personal Record Vault Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Personal Record Vault


Personal Record Vault does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer or any third party.

  What stays on your device
  - Workout logs, routines, body measurements, and progress photos are stored locally on your device.
  - Biometric authentication is processed entirely on-device by Android and is never transmitted.
  - Camera access is used solely to capture progress photos stored on your device.

  Optional Google Drive backup
  If you choose to back up your data, it is saved directly to your own Google Drive account. The developer has no access to your Drive. Your data is never sent to any developer-controlled
  server.

  No analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs
  This app contains no analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

  Last updated: May 2026
